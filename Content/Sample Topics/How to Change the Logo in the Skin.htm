Summary

Describes how to edit the logo in the toolbar of the WebHelp skin.

Full Description:

The logo for the WebHelp skin can be changed in the Skin Editor:

  1. In the Project Organizer, open the Skins folder.
  2. Double click the skin file that is associated with the WebHelp project.
  3. On the left hand side of the Skin Editor, click on the Styles tab.
  4. Under Styles, expand the "ToolbarItem" style.
  5. Highlight the Logo style.
  6. On the right hand side, expand Basic.
  7. Click directly to the right of the Icon property and browse for the new logo.
  8. After the new logo is selected, save the Skin file.
