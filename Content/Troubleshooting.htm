Troubleshooting

The list of known issues and possible solutions:

Problem Cause Suggested solution
No or very few search results for my flight request 1. There are no flight for your destination or dates 1. Try to change the search parameters
2. The website does not provide flights to your destination 2. Try to search on other websites
My payment was rejected 1. Incorrect card details entered 1. If permitted by the website, repeat the payment attempt with correct card details
2. Insufficient funds on the credit card 2. Use another card with sufficient amount
3. Payment limits are set for the card 3. Raise the limits up to the amount of your flight ticket price, or use another card with corresponding limits
No electronic ticket on my e-mail 1. E-mail dispatching of the website works with some delay 1. Wait cca. 10 mins
2. The message got into spam 2. Check the spam folder
3. Problems with website 3. Address the website support
