Buying procedure

 

Procedure overview

Buying a flight ticket online requires the following steps:

  1. Searching for available flights
  2. Choosing and booking a specific flight offer
  3. Payment
  4. Obtaining an electronic ticket

 

 

Step 1. Searching for available flights

1.1. Make up your mind regarding the required flight details:

  1. starting and destination points (cities or airports)
  2. one-way or return ticket
  3. date of flight (for one-way ticket) / dates (for return ticket)
  4. number of passengers

1.2. Go to an online flight search platform

E.g. skyscanner.net, kayak.com or a website of preferred airline company

1.3. Enter the required flight detail into search fields

1.4. Press 'Search'

1.5. Browse the search results

1.6. If provided by website – use filters to display only offers meeting the specific needs

E.g. only direct flights, tickets meeting the desired price range, etc.

1.7. Choose a desired offer by clicking on it

 

 

Step 2. Choosing and booking a specific flight offer

2.1. View carefully the offer details

2.2. Proceed to booking form

2.3. Enter the requested personal details (may differ depending on the website)

E.g. passenger names, dates of birth, passport details, e-mail, etc.

2.4. Check the entered details

Warning: in majority of cases you will not be able to modify your personal details after submitting the booking, or modifications may be subject to additional charges. Make sure to enter all the required details correctly

2.5. Proceed to payment

 

 

Step 3. Payment

3.1. Enter the required credit card details

  1. card number (16 digits)
  2. card holder’s name (if required)
  3. expiration date
  4. CVC code

2. Confirm your payment intention

3. If required by your bank – enter additional confirmation code (may be sent by SMS)

4. Wait for successful authorization of your payment by payment system

5. Follow further instruction of the website depending upon payment authorization result

Warning: some website may restrict the time within which the offer is blocked to enable the customer to enter the card details and make payment. Upon expiration of this time the offer may become unavailable

 

 

Step. 4 Obtaining an electronic ticket

After successful payment the website is supposed to send a message onto the e-mail which you provided on step 2 containing:

  1. payment confirmation or payment receipt
  2. electronic ticket
  3. (optionally) additional information about the flight (check-in conditions, luggage limitations, etc.)

 

4.1. Check your e-mail.

4.2. Save or print the electronic ticket.

4.3. Show the electronic ticket at the airport at check-in
